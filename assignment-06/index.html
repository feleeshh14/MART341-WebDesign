Coming Up Roses Tea

Ingredients
  • Rooibos
  • Rama basil
  • Lemon verbena
  • Chamomile
  • Rose
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon
  • Lavender
  • Peppercorn
  • Cardamom

Directions
  1. Place loose leaf tea in a mug of your choice using a tea bag or
    a tea strainer of your choice.
  2. Heat water and bring to a boil. This should take 3-4 minutes.
  3. Steep for 5-10 minutes and enjoy.
